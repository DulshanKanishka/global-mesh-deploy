Our Services

Internal Radio Operators

Carry your message directly to your audience with our dedicated online radio channels. From the success of "Food City Radio," we have expanded our services to supermarkets, restaurants, merchandisers, banks, and more. Have your own radio channel to entertain and update your valuable customers.

Movie Post Production Service

Since 2011, our Screen Works post-production unit has been a total service provider to the Sri Lankan movie industry. From editing to dubbing, color grading to 5.1 mixing, we offer comprehensive post production facilities. Our commitment to innovation led us to introduce 2k DCP for Digital projection systems in Sri Lanka.

Movie Exhibitors

With a heritage in film exhibition since 1956, we've been a pioneering force. From New Janaki Cinema to City Cinema's digital 3D cinema under the mini-cinema concept, we've consistently brought the latest in entertainment to our audiences. This journey further expanded with the opening of City Cinema Mount Lavinia in 2015 and City Cinema Kochchikade in 2018, continuing our commitment to delivering exceptional cinematic experiences.

SHOWZS App

Introducing "SHOWZS," a mobile app catering to Sri Lankan migrants worldwide. Enjoy brand new movies, stage dramas, live music shows, exclusive interviews, and more. GMS ensures you never miss out on local movies and entertainment events.

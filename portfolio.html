Our Creations in Cinema and Television

Hero Nero (2016)

Hero Nero (2016) is a Sri Lankan family drama directed and written by Pradeep Mahesh Liyanage. The story follows Ravindu and his friends as they bravely stand up against Lionel, a man attempting to have stray dogs, including their beloved dog Nero, captured by dog catchers. Determined to protect the animals, the children seek the help of media outlets to bring attention to the issue. Their efforts are further supported by Otara and Sangeetha, two compassionate figures who join the mission to rescue the dogs and ensure their safety.

Featuring performances by Duleeka Marapana, Mahinda Pathirage, and Pavith Thanura, Hero Nero delivers a heartfelt message about kindness, animal rights, and standing up for what is right.

Sara (2011)

Sara (2010) is a Sri Lankan drama film directed by Nishantha Pradeep and written by Pubudu Chathuranga. The story centers around Sara, highlighting her struggles, relationships, and emotional journey through life's challenges. Featuring performances by Sujani Menaka, Pubudu Chathuranga, and Maureen Charuni, the film explores themes of love, hardship, and resilience.

Maa (2025)

Maa is a compelling 8:30 PM teledrama on Swarnawahini that tells the story of a young beggar who has lost everything in life. One day, he comes across a man who looks exactly like him — same face, same build — who suddenly collapses from a heart attack inside a car. Seizing the moment, the beggar switches identities by wearing his clothes and stepping into his life.

What follows is a powerful journey of survival, identity, and how fate can turn one man’s tragedy into another’s opportunity.

Wow මැඩම් (2011)

Wow Madam is a 144-episode family comedy teledrama that aired from 6th May 2024 to 29th November 2024. Known for its light-hearted humor, clever dialogues, and engaging characters, the drama captured the attention of a wide audience throughout its run.

With its consistent comedic tone and entertaining situations, Wow Madam became a popular evening favorite, offering viewers a fun and relaxing experience.

Rayak ho Payak(රැයක් හෝ පැයක්)

Rayak Ho Payak is an intense psychological drama that explores power dynamics, moral boundaries, and the misuse of authority in a corporate setting. The story centers on a company boss who begins to pressure his secretary into compromising situations, challenging her dignity and values. As the plot unfolds, it reveals the emotional toll, ethical conflicts, and the silent battles faced by individuals caught in such positions.

This drama sheds light on sensitive social issues and invites viewers to reflect on justice, consent, and respect in the workplace.
